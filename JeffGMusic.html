Jeff Guernsey has performed across the US, in Japan, and the UK in clubs and arenas of all sizes. The multi-instrumentalist toured with Vince Gill and Steve Wariner for many years, performing on Gill's Gold record "High Lonesome Sound", and many other albums and projects. Guernsey started performing professionally as a teenager, touring the region and honing his skills on guitar, fiddle, and mandolin. He worked through the contest circuit, making lifelong friends who would ascend in the world of music as well.

He currently plays regular gigs, is a contest judge, and teachers a full schedule of students.
